About Alan Rich

Alan Rich is a Front-End Developer specializing in React.js with a background in Chemistry. Originally from Chicago, Alan is now based in Paris, France. He graduated from Benedictine University with a degree in Chemistry and published a thesis titled "A One-Pot Asymmetric Robinson Annulation in the Organic Chemistry Majors Laboratory" in the Journal of Chemical Education.

Alan has worked for companies such as Upwork, OC Tanner, and Research Innovations Inc as a Front-End Developer, and has experience working with JavaScript, Typescript React.js, Nx, MUI, and web technologies. He also previously worked for Nestlé as a chemist in their chocolate division.

Published Work

Alan's thesis, titled "A One-Pot Asymmetric Robinson Annulation in the Organic Chemistry Majors Laboratory," was published in the Journal of Chemical Education, 85 (2008). You can read the full article at DOI: 10.1021/ed085p1531.
